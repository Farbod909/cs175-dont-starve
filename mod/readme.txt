To install the mod, just copy it to Malmo\Minecraft\run\mods

If it's working properly, it will be in the list of mods from the main menu when you launch the game.

Effects:
Wheat requires 1 or 2 neighbors in either north/south or east/west, but not both (it only grows in lines)
Carrots require 0 or 1 neighbors
Potatoes require 2 neighbors in an L shape or 3 neighbors
Beetroots require 4+ neighbors including diagonals
